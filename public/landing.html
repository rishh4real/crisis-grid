How It Works

An automated, rapid-response pipeline scaling ground-level data to centralized dashboards instantly.

🎙️

1. Field Collection

Volunteers and workers upload raw situational audio, video, or text reports over low-bandwidth constraints directly from the crisis zone.

🤖

2. AI Extraction

Advanced NLP precisely extrapolates structured JSON intelligence—including exact locations, core needs, urgency, and estimated populace impacts.

🗺️

3. Live Dispatch

Coordinates are geoded and surfaced automatically to the main dispatch panel to facilitate instantaneous deployment prioritization.

Who is it for?

👷

Field Workers

Streamline reporting without filling out heavy forms. Fast audio transcription saves crucial time under pressure.

📡

NGO Coordinators

Get a top-level picture instantly. See exactly where resources are most needed without manually parsing fragmented texts.

🏢

Government Teams

Aggregate cross-agency intelligence into a single, reliable operational picture to deploy NDRF or military forces efficiently.
